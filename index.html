OUR SIX WEEK PROGRAM

Unprecedented Access. Authentic Connections. Unforgettable Experience.

With both established and emerging leaders selected, we meet weekly for six weeks. Each weekly session is designed around one faculty member, starting with one hour facilitated and led by them. Afterward, emerging leaders break into small groups for the second hour to discuss the content of the day, making applications and sharing what they learned. For the third and final hour of the session, Home Teams gather together. Each week, one individual takes a turn sharing a difficult personal or professional issue with the group. Their team members ask questions and weigh in to help them move forward or reach resolution. The vulnerability of Home Teams form strong bonds that last well beyond the six weeks—many choose to stay in touch outside of the program.
